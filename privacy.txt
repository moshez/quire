Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

---

Overview

This app is designed to respect your privacy. We collect no personal information and have no interest in knowing who you are or what you read.

---

Analytics

We may collect anonymized usage analytics to understand how the app is used — for example, which features are used most, what screen sizes are common, and where people encounter errors. Analytics are collected without any account, name, or email attached. IP addresses are not retained.

---

What we do not collect

- Your identity — no name, email, or account
- Your library — no titles, authors, or content
- Your reading activity — no history, notes, highlights, or bookmarks
- Your precise location

What you read is your business.
